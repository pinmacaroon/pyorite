usages

Handcannon

the handcannon is a long range weapon with infinite durability. to fire it, the user must hold a valid ammunition item (data driven via pyorite:items/gun_ammo item tag), for example shwrappel in their offhand. after that, right click with it equipped to fire in the direction of sight. in survival, it has a cooldown and consumes one offhand item.

Aeroblade

the aeroblade is a close-range sword, wich while equipped gives the user slow falling. on right click, it dashes the player forward, with a cooldown.

Plushies

the three creator plushie blocks are non colliding, rotatable blocks. they drop themselves when broken. on right click, they play a custom sound, display particles and look cute. when below 3 hearts of health, a right click heals 2 hearts.

Pretty Pink Heart

the main component of the plushies, the item can be found as an enderman drop or as a wandering trader trade.

Elementite Smiting Template

this template can be found in ancient city chests.

Aeroite

this is the main component of the Aeroblade , it can be found in buried treasure chests.
